                Settings

                Theme

                Choose a built-in theme or one you have created. The theme cycles with Ctrl+,.

                Editor

                Or use Ctrl+= / Ctrl+- / Ctrl+0 in the editor.

                Note list

                Pinned notes always come first within the chosen sort. You can drag pinned notes in the sidebar to reorder them manually.

                Language

                Mycelium ships English baseline strings. Other locales are community-contributed; drop a translations.json in the data folder (override entries by key). Full string coverage rolls out in upcoming releases.

                Privacy & lock

                Loading...

                When the workspace lock is enabled, the app starts on a passphrase prompt and every note is encrypted at rest with ChaCha20-Poly1305 (key derived from your passphrase via 50,000 iterations of BLAKE3). With a strong passphrase, even disk-level access cannot read your notes without it. Lose the passphrase = lose the data.

                    Templates

                    Templates let you spin up a new note pre-populated with structure (meeting agenda, daily log, project brief, ...). Save the currently-open note as a template, or create a new note from any template you've saved. Use {{date}}, {{time}}, {{datetime}}, {{title}}, {{year}}, {{month}}, {{day}} as substitution tokens; {{cursor}} places the caret at that spot after expansion.

                      Import & export

                      Imported files become new notes; their first # heading becomes the title. The multi-file picker batches them. Export gives you one .md per note as a zip-like JSON bundle you can download.

                      Backup & restore

                      A workspace backup is one JSON file containing every note, theme, template and your settings. Use it to migrate to another machine, archive a snapshot in time, or recover after a disk failure.

                      Storage

                      Notes, themes, plugins, templates and history live in %APPDATA%\Mycelium\.

                        Activity (last 12 weeks)

                        Note graph

                        Notes connected by [[wiki-link]]. Drag the canvas; click a node to open the note.

                        Kanban board

                        Group every note by a frontmatter property (e.g. status: doing). Drag a card between columns to rewrite that note's frontmatter. Notes without the chosen property go to (none).

                        Month calendar

                        Notes carrying a frontmatter date property (default due) appear pinned to their day. Click a note in a cell to open it. Click an empty cell to filter the sidebar to all notes due that day.

                        Text snippets

                        In the editor body, type ;name followed by Tab to expand the snippet at the caret. Keys must be alphanumeric / - / _.
