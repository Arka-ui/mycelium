          Privacy & lock

          Loading...

          When the workspace lock is enabled, the app starts on a passphrase prompt and every note is encrypted at rest with ChaCha20-Poly1305 (key derived from your passphrase via 50,000 iterations of BLAKE3). With a strong passphrase, even disk-level access cannot read your notes without it. Lose the passphrase = lose the data.

              Templates

              Templates let you spin up a new note pre-populated with structure (meeting agenda, daily log, project brief, ...). Save the currently-open note as a template, or create a new note from any template you've saved.

                Import & export

                Imported files become new notes; their first # heading becomes the title. Export gives you one .md per note as a zip-like JSON bundle you can download.

                Backup & restore

                A workspace backup is one JSON file containing every note, theme, template and your settings. Use it to migrate to another machine, archive a snapshot in time, or recover after a disk failure.

                Storage

                Notes, themes, plugins, templates and history live in %APPDATA%\Mycelium\.
